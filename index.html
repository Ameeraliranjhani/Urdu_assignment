About

This project is dedicated to spreading awareness about the benefits, growth, and nurturing of Neem trees. Join us as we plant, protect, and promote a greener future for generations to come through education, community engagement, and environmental action.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a green revolution by empowering communities to plant and protect Neem trees, preserving natural health solutions and fighting climate change at a grassroots level.

Our Team

We are a diverse group of botanists, environmentalists, educators, and community leaders united by our love for nature and our belief in sustainable living. Together, we build a future rooted in green values.
